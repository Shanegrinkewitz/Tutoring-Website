SAT Preparation

If your child plans to take the SAT, it is a good idea to hire a tutor because it is almost certain that your child will receive a better score as a result. An SAT score can make the difference of which college a student is accepted into. Our tutors can help students prepare for the math section, the reading and writing section, and the essay. The goal of our tutors will be to maximize the student's score.

ACT Preparation

If your child plans to take the ACT, you can contact us to ensure that he or she gets as much preparation as possible. Like the SAT, the ACT is an important factor that can make the difference of which college a student is accepted into. Our tutors can help students prepare for the math section, the English section, the reading section, and the science section. As always, the goal of our tutors will be to maximize the student's score on this very important test.
